Why a percentage

A commitment they can keep in every season.

A fixed amount is a guess: too heavy in a lean month, quietly left behind after a raise. A percentage is never a guess. It stays in proportion to what actually came in.

When income rises, the gift rises. When a season is lean, the gift eases down on its own. Nothing for anyone to edit.

$25 $50 $100 → 10%
